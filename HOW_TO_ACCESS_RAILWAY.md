# 🚂 How to Access Your Railway Deployment

## ✅ GOOD NEWS: Your App is Already Live Globally!

When Railway showed "deployed successfully," your app became available to **anyone in the world** with internet access.

---

## 🔍 Find Your Global URL (3 Simple Steps)

### Step 1: Open Railway
- Go to: **https://railway.app**
- Log in with your account

### Step 2: Click Your Project
- You'll see your project in the dashboard
- Click on it to open

### Step 3: Get the URL

**Option A - In Settings:**
1. Click **"Settings"** tab at the top
2. Scroll to **"Networking"** or **"Domains"** section
3. You'll see a URL like: `https://web-production-xxxx.up.railway.app`
4. If you don't see a URL, click **"Generate Domain"** button
5. Copy the URL

**Option B - In Deployments:**
1. Click **"Deployments"** tab
2. Click on your latest deployment (shows "Success")
3. Look for the URL at the top or in deployment details
4. Copy it

## 🆚 Difference Between Local and Railway

| Feature | Local (localhost:5173) | Railway (Global) |
|---------|------------------------|------------------|
| **Access** | Only you, on your computer | Anyone, anywhere |
| **URL** | http://localhost:5173 | https://your-app.up.railway.app |
| **Availability** | Only when running | 24/7 always on |
| **Internet needed** | No | Yes |
| **Share with others** | ❌ No | ✅ Yes |

## ❓ Troubleshooting

**Q: I can't find the URL**
- Look in Settings → Domains section
- Or Deployments → Click latest deployment

**Q: No domain is shown**
- Click "Generate Domain" button in Settings

**Q: URL shows error**
- Check deployment logs for errors
- Ensure build completed successfully

**Q: Shows "Backend is running" message**
- Frontend wasn't built during deployment
- Need to configure build command in Railway settings
